Futurama

After being frozen in the year 1999, pizza delivery guy Fry wakes up in the 31st century and joins an intergalactic delivery crew. Along with his new robot friend Bender and his cyclops captain Leela, they travel to distant worlds having adventures, encountering aliens and mutants, and occasionally delivering things.

Futurama is an animated series created by Matt Groening which first aired in 1999. It's set in the year 3000 in the city of New New York. It ran for 5 years before being canceled in 2003, revived in 2008 with four feature length movies, and followed by another four seasons before being canceled again in 2013. The series has been recognized with many awards throughout it's run and is considered one of the best animated series of all time.
